Heading to PyData

By Tait Clarridge, Thu 01 May 2014, Category Announcements

announcement

So I am one of the lucky few from the Engineering team @ Chango chosen to go and check out PyData down in San Francisco!

Our lead large scale data architect/senior VP of dealing with epic amounts of data Tim Spurway (@t1m) will be giving a talk on Hustle tomorrow, so if you are headed down I highly recommend sitting in and watching this column oriented database plough through massive data sets with speed and ease.

Time to get in line at the airport, but really excited for my first visit to California!
